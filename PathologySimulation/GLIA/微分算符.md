# ∇ 微分算符
$$∇ = (\frac{\partial }{\partial x},\frac{\partial }{\partial y},\frac{\partial }{\partial z})$$
## 梯度 grad
==对标量场计算得向量==
> 表示标量场增长最快的方向和速率
$$grad f(x,y,z)= \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\cdot i +\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\cdot j+\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\cdot k$$
$$grad f= \nabla f$$
## 散度 div

^dc7fd1

==对向量场计算得标量==
> 表示某点像”源”向外发散还是像“汇”向内聚集
$$div A= \frac{\partial P}{\partial x} +\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial R}{\partial z}$$
$$div \vec F= \nabla \cdot \vec{F}$$
## 旋度 rot
==对向量场计算得向量==
> 表示向量场在某点附近是否有局部旋转趋势 & 绕哪个轴旋转

$$  
\nabla \times \vec F  
=  
\begin{vmatrix}  
i & j & k \\  
\frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\  
P & Q & R  
\end{vmatrix}  
$$$$  
rot\ \vec F  
=  
\nabla \times \vec F  
$$
